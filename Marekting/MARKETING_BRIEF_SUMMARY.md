## Positioning

This website is not an app-login landing page. It is the public face of an OEM-ready IoT platform for:

- device manufacturers
- OEM customers
- system integrators
- enterprises
- approved third-party device partners

Core message:

> Smart One by Jenix helps businesses build, connect, provision, manage and scale IoT products from one cloud, web and mobile platform.

## Primary Website Goals

- Establish Smart One as a credible IoT platform brand
- Satisfy Google OAuth homepage expectations
- Support Google Play policy readiness
- Convert visitors into OEM, API, integration, or sales enquiries
- Explain platform capability without exposing private infrastructure details
